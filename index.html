About Me

Hello! My name is Paweł Tokarczyk, and I am a passionate software developer and technology enthusiast from Limanowa. I have been immersed in the world of coding and technology since my teenage years, and over time, this hobby has transformed into a fulfilling career. My journey into the tech world began with a fascination for how things work, and it was this curiosity that led me to explore programming languages and software development.

I pursued my formal education in Computer Science, where I gained a solid foundation in various programming languages, algorithms, and data structures. My academic journey provided me with the theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary to excel in the field of software development. During my studies, I was particularly drawn to the fields of web development and game development, which allowed me to combine my technical skills with my creative interests.

Over the years, I have worked on numerous projects, ranging from simple websites to complex software applications. Each project has been an opportunity to learn and grow, pushing the boundaries of what I thought was possible. My experience includes working with languages and frameworks such as C#, JavaScript, Angular, React, and C++. I have a deep understanding of both front-end and back-end development, allowing me to create seamless and efficient applications.

In addition to my technical skills, I pride myself on my creativity, cooperation skills, punctuality, and patience. These attributes have not only helped me succeed in my professional endeavors but have also made me a valuable team member in collaborative projects. I believe that the best solutions come from diverse perspectives and open communication, and I strive to foster these principles in every team I work with.

Apart from my professional life, I have a wide range of interests that keep me inspired and motivated. I enjoy coding not just as a profession but also as a hobby, constantly exploring new technologies and frameworks. Game development is another passion of mine, as it allows me to blend my love for storytelling and technology. I also have a keen interest in video editing, which has taught me the importance of attention to detail and creativity.
